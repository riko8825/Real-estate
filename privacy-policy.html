Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our site or get in touch.

Effective date: 19 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 19 May 2026

Your Holiday Villas Pattaya ("we," "us," "our") operates the website pridaproperty.com. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have under Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where it applies to you.

1. Who we are

Your Holiday Villas Pattaya is a real estate agency based in Pratumnak Hill, Pattaya, Chonburi 20150, Thailand. We help international and local clients buy, sell, and rent pool villas, condos, and houses across Pattaya.

2. What data we collect

2.1 Information you give us

When you submit our contact form, send an email, or message us on WhatsApp or LINE, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address and / or phone number
  • The content of your message (property of interest, budget, intent to buy / rent / sell)
  • Any other details you voluntarily share

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you browse our site, the following may be collected automatically by our hosting provider or by cookies you consent to:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on each page
  • Referring website and approximate location (city / country level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • IP address (in anonymised form where possible)

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Responding to enquiries — answering your questions, sending property suggestions, scheduling viewings
  • Property transactions — facilitating purchase, sale, or rental of property you have engaged us about
  • Legal and tax obligations — Thai property law requires identification and source-of-funds documentation for buyer transactions
  • Site improvement — understanding which pages and properties attract interest (only with your cookie consent)
  • Marketing — sending you new listings only if you have explicitly opted in

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

4. Legal basis (GDPR Article 6)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for marketing emails and non-essential cookies (analytics, advertising)
  • Contract — for processing your enquiry and any subsequent transaction
  • Legal obligation — for KYC, anti-money-laundering, and Thai property registration requirements
  • Legitimate interest — for responding to enquiries you initiated and protecting our website from abuse

5. Cookies and tracking

Our site uses a cookie consent banner powered by Silktide Consent Manager. You can manage your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie icon in the bottom-left corner of any page.

5.1 Necessary cookies (always active)

These store your consent choice and any session state required for the site to function. They cannot be disabled.

5.2 Analytics cookies (opt-in)

If you accept analytics cookies, we may use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate visitor behaviour. No personal identifiers are collected without your explicit consent. As of the effective date of this policy, analytics are not active on this site — you will be informed here when they are.

5.3 Advertising cookies (opt-in)

If enabled in the future, advertising cookies would be used for remarketing. They are currently not active.

6. Data sharing and third parties

We share your data only with parties strictly necessary to fulfil your enquiry or our legal obligations:

  • Hosting provider — Vercel Inc. (USA), which hosts pridaproperty.com
  • Communication platforms — WhatsApp (Meta Platforms, Ireland) and LINE Corporation (Japan) if you choose to contact us via those channels; you are subject to their own privacy policies
  • Thai legal and tax authorities — when required by law for a property transaction (Land Department, Revenue Department)
  • Legal partners — our retained Thai property law firm, only when you become a client and only with your knowledge

We will not transfer your data to any other third party without your explicit consent.

7. Data retention

  • Enquiry data — kept for 24 months from your last contact, then deleted
  • Transaction records — kept for 10 years as required by Thai accounting and tax law
  • Cookie consent records — kept for 12 months, then you are re-prompted

8. Your rights

Under PDPA (Thailand) and GDPR (EU), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectify — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erase — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Restrict — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — to processing based on legitimate interest or for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where consent is the legal basis
  • Lodge a complaint — with Thailand's PDPC or your local EU supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at chabaratree@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data security

We use HTTPS encryption for all site traffic, secure email providers, and limit access to your data to staff who genuinely need it. No system is 100% secure — but we take this seriously and act quickly if we ever detect a breach. In the event of a personal data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and the relevant authority as required by law.

10. International transfers

Our hosting (Vercel) is based in the USA. Our communication tools (WhatsApp, LINE) may also process data outside Thailand and the EU. Where required by GDPR, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

11. Children

Our site and services are intended for adults aged 18 or over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted at the top of this page. The "Last updated" date at the start of this document always reflects the current version.
